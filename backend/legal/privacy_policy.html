Lancely

Privacy Policy

Effective: June 15, 2026   Last updated: June 15, 2026

Lancely (“we”, “our”, or “us”) builds a mobile app that helps freelancers and solo professionals manage clients, quotes, projects, invoices, and payments. We take your privacy seriously and have designed Lancely to collect the minimum information needed to make the product work — nothing more.

This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data. By using Lancely, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google (via Emergent-managed OAuth), we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from Google. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Business data you enter. Client contacts, project notes, quotes, invoices, payment records, business name, logo (optional), and accent color. This data is yours; we treat it as confidential.
  • Subscription information. If you upgrade to Lancely Pro, the App Store / Google Play handle payment. We receive a subscription status flag (active / inactive) via RevenueCat — we never see your card or bank details.

1.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Session tokens. A random session token is stored on your device to keep you signed in. It contains no personal data.
  • Basic error logs. If the app crashes or an API request fails, our backend logs the HTTP method, endpoint, and timestamp so we can fix bugs. Logs do not include the contents of your business data.
  • Device info. Standard request metadata such as IP address (from your network provider) is processed by our hosting provider to deliver the service. We do not store this for analytics.

1.3 What we do NOT collect

  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, or health data unless you explicitly initiate an action that requires it (e.g. uploading a logo from your photo library).
  • We do not use any third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not train any AI or machine-learning model on your business data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in securely.
  • To provide the core features of Lancely — saving and displaying your clients, quotes, projects, invoices, and payments.
  • To generate PDFs for quotes and invoices, on-device or server-side, branded with your business name and logo (if you choose to provide one).
  • To send you essential service emails (e.g. account-related, security, billing receipts). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To debug, secure, and improve the app — for example, identifying crashes or unauthorized access attempts.
  • To process subscriptions and grant Pro entitlements.

3. How We Share Information

We share data only with the limited service providers required to run Lancely, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Google Sign-In. Used solely to verify your identity. Google’s privacy policy applies to data they collect: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play. When you purchase Lancely Pro, your payment is handled by Apple or Google under their respective terms. We never see your payment details.
  • RevenueCat. Manages your subscription status across platforms. RevenueCat receives an anonymized user ID and your purchase events. See revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Cloud hosting (MongoDB & Emergent infrastructure). Used to store your data securely. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

We will also disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of Lancely, our users, or the public.

4. Data Retention

We keep your business data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data — clients, projects, quotes, invoices, payments, logo, and settings — is permanently deleted from our production database within 30 days. Backups are rotated and overwritten within 90 days.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under GDPR (EU), UK GDPR, CCPA (California), and similar laws, including:

  • Access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction. Update or correct inaccurate information directly within the app.
  • Deletion. Delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • Portability. Export your invoices, clients, and payments as CSV from Settings → Reports.
  • Objection / Restriction. Object to certain processing or ask us to restrict it.
  • Withdraw consent. Stop using Lancely; uninstall the app; revoke Google access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

To exercise any right, email privacy@lancely.app. We respond within 30 days.

6. Children

Lancely is built for adults running a business. It is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used Lancely, please contact us and we will remove the data.

7. Security

We use industry-standard practices: TLS encryption for data in transit, encryption at rest, hashed session tokens, scoped API authorization, and least-privilege database access. No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your data and notify you of any material breach as required by law.

8. International Transfers

Lancely is operated from servers that may be located outside your country. If you access Lancely from the EEA, UK, or other regions with data transfer rules, by using the app you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in those server locations.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated in-app or by email. Continued use of Lancely after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
